<font size=3>人言《<a href="https://www.baike.com/wikiid/2878271293740600013?prd=result_list&view_id=5avtvkf0dgs000">扬州慢</a>》有<a href="https://www.baike.com/wikiid/4936918054078796917?prd=result_list&view_id=2n25owx54v4000">黍离之悲</a>。</font>

<br>

寒水初团，晚云[澌没](https://www.baike.com/wikiid/6487848847966593691?prd=home_search&search_id=19z2jym8ujvk0&view_id=53np1pj639s000)，远林漠漠如纱。望垂天压地，裹百里千家。几多是、归来[倦客](https://www.baike.com/wikiid/9102214718558320990?prd=result_list&view_id=22y7p4gi3sn400)，避人无计，闲数飞鸦。者良辰，封舍埋田，楼隐悲笳。

[首丘](https://www.baike.com/wikiid/5105660619724440636?prd=home_search&search_id=1r8ytdupr08000&view_id=2qqqf81e31q000)尚死，又何须、[多难](https://www.baike.com/wikiid/1054320940205180646?prd=result_list&view_id=598gt7jvai4000)堪夸。恰故国城春，零星草木，山海无华。更那故人长绝，谁相与、学种[侯瓜](https://www.baike.com/wikiid/1859333987684886542?prd=result_list&view_id=1jiqnwe1xhq800)。算年来踪迹，终成岁月[清嘉](https://www.baike.com/wikiid/1861915537866676433?prd=home_search&search_id=4tm4gmciez2000&view_id=3gjcrreiedw000)。
